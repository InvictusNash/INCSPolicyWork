Policy & Operations Alignment Project

The Policy & Operations Alignment Project is a comprehensive initiative to organize, standardize, and maintain the policies, procedures, systems, and operational documentation that govern Invictus Nashville Charter School.

This project is intended to ensure alignment across the school's responsibilities as a Tennessee public charter school, an employer, and a licensed preschool program, while providing a centralized resource for operational guidance, compliance documentation, and institutional knowledge.
